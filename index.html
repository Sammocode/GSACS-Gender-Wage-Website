Gender Pay Gap Issues

Why is Gender Wage Gap an Issue?

On average, Palestinian women make 27% less money than Palestinian males. Because of their differences in gender, men and women will always get paid differently from one another, regardless of whether they have the same occupations. Many women in the workforce work harder than men do because of the difficulties they encounter at work. They also deal with unequal opportunities and find it challenging to bridge career gaps by returning to the profession. Despite all of the challenges they confront at work, women consistently put in more effort than men.

In actuality, there are over 7,000 firms owned and run by women in Palestine, compared to 16% of males of working age. Only 3.5% of women in Palestine between the ages of 15 and 64 set up their own businesses. These women put forth a lot of effort to get the money they deserve.
