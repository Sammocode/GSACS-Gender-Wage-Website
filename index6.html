About us!

We are students at Guttman Community College. Our majors are Information Technology. Throughout this assignment we got to learn and get in depth about the gender wage gap and how it is affecting women who are in the workforce. We also talked about solutions that should be given or recommended that can help fix the wage issues.

The Overall Outcome of the Wage Gap

The wage gap is not fair to women since they earn less than men do. Now in today's time women would go to school and get many or more higher degrees in college to be able to earn as much money in their future career as they can. Women and men should both be paid equal so that everyone can get the same rate of pay as other employees as well. As a society we hope that in the future same wages should be given no matter what the career is. The future is getting more and more expensive. With no equal pay people will have struggles and living situations as well.
